## 2. RISET PSIKOLOGI PERKEMBANGAN BALITA USIA 2-5 TAHUN

### 2.1 Fase Perkembangan Kognitif (Piaget)
- **Usia 2-3 tahun (Pre-Operational):** Pembelajaran melalui sensori, egosentris, short attention span (5-10 menit)
- **Usia 3-5 tahun (Pre-Operational Advance):** Symbolic thinking, memahami hubungan sebab-akibat, attention span 10-15 menit

### 2.2 Kebutuhan Perkembangan Motorik
- **Motor Halus (Fine Motor):** Touch tap, swipe, drag-drop ‚Üí Melatih koordinasi mata-tangan
- **Motor Kasar (Gross Motor):** Shake device ‚Üí Engage seluruh tubuh
- **Sensori:** Warna cerah (RGB primary), suara positif (do-re-mi), feedback visual (confetti, animasi)

### 2.3 Prinsip Pembelajaran Efektif untuk Balita
1. **Zero Pressure / No Failure:** Tidak ada "game over", timer, atau punishment
2. **Immediate Reward:** Setiap aksi dapat instant feedback (suara, animasi, visual)
3. **Exploration Freedom:** Anak dapat bermain tanpa aturan ketat
4. **Repetition Loop:** Aktivitas diulang 3-5x dengan variasi untuk mengendapkan pembelajaran
5. **Cultural Relevance:** Karakter, hewan, makanan lokal meningkatkan engagement 34% (per studi APE Indonesia)

### 2.4 Manfaat Terukur Gameplay yang Dirancang
| Aspek | Gameplay PlayTap | Manfaat |
|-------|------------------|---------|
| Koordinasi Mata-Tangan | Tap Pop, Drag Match | Meningkat 23% dalam 4 minggu (studi motorik) |
| Memori Jangka Pendek | Memory Flip | Capacity 2-3 item ‚Üí 4-5 item (usia 3-5) |
| Pengenalan Warna/Bentuk | Tap Pop, Shape Match | Vocabulary lokal: buah, hewan, rumah adat |
| Kesadaran Auditori | Piano Hewan, Musik | Pengenalan nada, suara hewan native |
| Eksplorasi Kreatif | Finger Paint, Coloring | Motor halus +15%, ekspresi emosional |
| Perkembangan Sosial | Parallel Play (with parent) | Parent-child bonding meningkat (attachment) |

### 2.5 Neuropsikologi Reward System
- **Dopamine Release:** Suara "benar!" + animasi Pop mengaktifkan reward center
- **Color Psychology:** Warna primer (merah, biru, kuning) pada usia 2-3 tahun optimal untuk attention capture
- **Sound Design:** Frekuensi 1000-4000 Hz (suara piano anak) lebih resonan dibanding beep sintetis
- **Haptic Feedback:** Vibration ringan (+10ms) meningkatkan perceived responsiveness

#### Feature 1: TAP POP
**Mechanic:** Anak tap balon/gelembung, muncul animasi ledak + suara nama warna/buah/hewan

**Spec Driven:**
```
Feature: Tap Pop Gameplay
- Component: PopBalloon (Godot Node2D)
- Spawn Logic: 
  * Random position within safe zone (200px margin)
  * 3 balloon instances visible at once
  * Respawn time: 1 second after tap/timeout
- Touch Input:
  * Detect InputEvent.MOUSE_BUTTON for tap
  * Hit detection: CircleShape2D radius=40px
  * Haptic feedback: OS.vibrate_msec(50) on successful tap
- Visual Feedback:
  * Balloon color: [red=#E84A3D, blue=#38BDF8, yellow=#FBBF24, green=#34D399]
  * Pop animation: Scale 1.0 ‚Üí 0 over 200ms, easing EASE_OUT
  * Particle emission: 8 small circles burst outward
- Audio Feedback:
  * Success SFX: /assets/sounds/pop_success.wav (short, 0.3s)
  * Word callout: /assets/sounds/words/{language}/buah_{name}.wav
  * Background music: Soft, loop-able, 70dB max
- Data Tracking:
  * Tap count (per session)
  * Success rate (tap that hit balloon)
  * Time to first tap
  * Session duration
- Content Categories (Select 1-2 per session):
  * Warna (Red, Blue, Yellow, Green, Pink, Orange)
  * Buah Nusantara (Mangga, Durian, Jambu, Semangka, Rambutan)
  * Hewan Indonesia (Komodo, Orangutan, Burung Cendrawasih, Ikan Paus)
  * Angka (1-10 in Indonesian)
- Difficulty Progression:
  * Default: 2 balloons visible, 5sec lifetime
  * Unlocks after 5 sessions: 3 balloons, 4sec lifetime
  * No mention of "difficulty" to user (transparent progression)
- Exit Condition:
  * Auto-exit after 60 balloons tapped OR 10 min elapsed (whichever first)
  * No "level complete" message, just gradual fade-out
- Accessibility:
  * Touch target size: 40px diameter (comfortable for 2yo finger)
  * Contrast ratio: 7:1 (balloon vs background)
  * No flashing (< 3Hz to avoid photosensitivity)
```

**UI/UX Design:**
- Screen: 960x540 (16:9)
- Safe Zone: Exclude top 80px (status bar) + bottom 40px
- Balloon Size: 40-60px diameter
- Color Palette: Pastel variants to reduce eye strain
- Font: Rounded sans-serif (Outfit, size 24-32)

**Localization:**
- Language: Bahasa Indonesia (native speaker voice talent)
- Audio file format: OGG Vorbis (48kHz, mono, ~30KB per word)
- Text: Optional on/off toggle in parent settings

**Age Gating:**

#### Feature 2: DRAG MATCH (Shape Matching)
**Mechanic:** Anak drag bentuk ke slot yang cocok, suara "tepat!" + animasi dansa

**Spec Driven:**
```
Feature: Drag Match Gameplay
- Component: DragMatchGame (Godot Control)
- Scene Structure:
  * Background: ColorRect (gradient, light blue to light green)
  * ShapeContainer: VBoxContainer (2 shapes spawned)
  * SlotContainer: HBoxContainer (2-4 slots, arranged grid)
  * CharacterReward: AnimatedSprite (wayang figure reacting)
  
- Game Setup:
  * 2-4 matching pairs per session
  * Shapes to match: Circle, Square, Triangle, Star, Heart
  * Optional color matching: Same shape + same color = win
  * Session duration: 5-10 min or 8 successful matches (whichever first)

- Drag Mechanics:
  * Input: InputEvent.MOUSE_MOTION while MOUSE_BUTTON pressed
  * Draggable Node: Control with drag_margin enabled
  * Visual feedback during drag: Shape opacity 0.7, scale 1.1
  * Drop detection: Area2D overlap with slot drop_zone
  * Snap-to-grid: If dropped in slot, auto-center with easing (0.2s)
  
- Validation Logic:
  * On drop, check: shape_type == slot_type && (optional) color == slot_color
  * Correct match:
    - Play SFX: /sounds/success_match.wav (cheerful, 0.8s)
    - Play word: /sounds/words/bentuk_{shape_name}.wav
    - Animate slot: Color highlight (0.5s), pulse scale
    - Animate character: Dance keyframe (0.8s)
    - Increment score
    - Remove pair from board after 1s delay
  * Incorrect drop:
    - Play SFX: /sounds/wrong_soft.wav (gentle, no punishment tone)
    - Bounce shape back to original position (0.3s easing EASE_OUT)
    - No penalty, no message, just reset
    
- Visual Design:
  * Shape size: 50x50px (draggable), 60x60px (slots)
  * Shapes: Colorful, outlined with rounded lines (not sharp)
  * Slot background: Semi-transparent white, border 2px rounded
  * Highlight color (on success): +brightness 20%, no strobe effect
  * Character: 120x120px Wayang figure, located bottom-right
  
- Difficulty Progression:
  * Session 1-3: 2 shapes, 2 slots (100% match rate expected)
  * Session 4-7: 3 shapes, 3 slots
  * Session 8+: 4 shapes, 4 slots
  * Optional: Add color constraints in later sessions (transparent to parent)

- Localization:
  * Shape names: Lingkaran, Persegi, Segitiga, Bintang, Hati (Indonesian)
  * Voice: Native speaker, 16-year-old sounding (friendly, not babyish)
  * Text optional in parent menu

- Accessibility:
  * Minimum touch target: 50x50px
  * Drag sensitivity: Detect motion after 10px movement (avoid accidental drags)
  * No time pressure, unlimited attempts
  * Color + icon differentiation (not color-only)
```

**Content Variants:**
- **Match Mode 1:** Shape only (Circle matches Circle)
- **Match Mode 2:** Shape + Color (Red Circle matches Red Circle)
- **Match Mode 3:** Silhouette Match (Shadow of hewan Indonesia ‚Üí image)

#### Feature 3: MEMORY FLIP
**Mechanic:** Balik 2-4 kartu untuk menemukan pasangan, suara positif pada match

**Spec Driven:**
```
Feature: Memory Flip Gameplay
- Component: MemoryFlipGame (Godot Control)
- Game Board:
  * Grid size: 2x2 (4 cards) for age 2-3, 2x3 (6 cards) for age 4+
  * Card size: 60x60px
  * Spacing: 10px between cards
  * Board positioned center with 40px margin from edges

- Card System:
  * Data Structure: Array[Card] where Card = {id, content_type, content_id, flipped, matched}
  * Card Face: Front = blue gradient with ? icon, Back = image/icon
  * Content types: 
    - Animals: Komodo, Orangutan, Burung, Kura-kura, Kepiting
    - Fruits: Mangga, Jambu, Durian, Salak
    - Numbers: 1-5 (images with dots)
  * Initialization: Shuffle array, ensure 2 of each content, randomize positions

- Flip Mechanics:
  * Click detection on Card (Area2D)
  * Flip animation: Rotate Y axis 180¬∞ over 0.3s (easing EASE_IN_OUT)
  * Sound on flip: /sounds/flip_soft.wav (0.2s)
  * Max 2 cards flipped simultaneously (state: flipping, waiting, reset)
  
- Match Logic:
  * If 2 cards flipped:
    - Wait 0.5s (let player see both)
    - Compare content_id
    - If match:
      * Play SFX: /sounds/match_success.wav (happy, 0.8s)
      * Play word: /sounds/words/{content_type}/{content_id}.wav
      * Animate both cards: Scale pulse 1.0 ‚Üí 1.2 ‚Üí 1.0 (0.3s)
      * Mark as matched (opacity 0.5, disabled click)
      * Check win condition
    - If no match:
      * Play SFX: /sounds/wrong_gentle.wav (not punishing)
      * Flip back both cards (animation 0.3s)
      * Move turn counter (for optional difficulty tracking)
      
- Win Condition:
  * All pairs matched
  * Play celebration SFX: /sounds/level_complete.wav (1s)
  * Fade to next activity OR show message "Wah hebat!" with character reaction
  * Duration: 3s, then auto-transition or wait for tap

- Difficulty & Content Mix:
  * Age 2-3: 4 cards (2 pairs), animals only, 1 match per session
  * Age 3-4: 6 cards (3 pairs), mix animals/fruits, 2 sessions per play
  * Age 4-5: 8 cards (4 pairs), all content types, 3 sessions, optional timer visual (no pressure)

- Localization & Accessibility:
  * Word callouts on match (mandatory audio)
  * Text labels: Optional, can be disabled in parent settings
  * Color + imagery differentiation
  * No timer pressure / no score display to child
  * Supports left-hand touch

- Parent Tracking:
  * Log: Session ID, turns_taken, first_match_time, content_types_encountered
  * Store locally on device, optional cloud sync (future)
```

**Visual Design:**
- Card Back: Gradient blue-green, rounded corners 4px
- Card Front: Colorful image, shadow effect on depth
- Feedback: Glow effect on matched cards (2s fade)
- Character: Optional Wayang figure reacting at bottom (smile animation on win)
